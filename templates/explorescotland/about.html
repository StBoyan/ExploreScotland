This project has been put together by:
Boyan Stoynov, Denitsa Velichkova & Diliyana Savcheva
for the ITECH module.
It's purpose is to educate kids about Scotland.

Our Initiative:

Join our initiative! Nowadays our kids are exposed to the new technological innovations and we cannot stop them from using the perks of the market. Most of the applications available are repetitive in functionaries and they are not educative. We want to be different and we designed an application, which is going to engage the new generations with valuable information and develop their learning skills. Your kid will be presented with a map with different signs on it which will represent different information about the places in Scotland. Then they can check what they have learned by talking to/taking quizzes from their virtual friends Scott and Lily. Our virtual personas are designed to test your kids knowledge, acquired from exploring the interactive map. Since the kids are young to subscribe to an online application you are the only one who can sign them up and check their progress(levels).
